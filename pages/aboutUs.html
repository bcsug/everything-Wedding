The Problem We Solved

Born from personal experience, built on professional expertise

While planning my own wedding, I experienced firsthand the frustration of constantly chasing vendors instead of them proactively managing their responsibilities. The stress of follow-ups, the uncertainty of quality, and the lack of accountability inspired me to create a better way.

Everything Wedding was born from this personal pain point, combined with 13 years of project management experience in telecommunications. We apply professional project management principles to wedding planning, ensuring vendors deliver excellence while couples enjoy their journey to "I do."
